OUR MISSION

Welcome to the Center for Physical Therapy and Sports Medicine, where compassionate care meets individualized healing. With almost two decades of service as a family-owned outpatient clinic, we're more than practitioners – we're partners on your path to recovery.

Led by a husband and wife team, our legacy thrives on personalized attention. We understand that your goals are as unique as your journey, and we're dedicated to crafting tailored solutions for musculoskeletal issues and injuries.

At the heart of our practice is your success story. Athletes pursuing excellence, individuals regaining mobility – we're here to guide every ambition. Through expert evaluation and custom treatment plans, we're committed to restoring your body's potential.

Our services

Our physical therapy treatments focus on healing or rehabilitating your musculoskeletal injury or disorder to restore function and return to normal activities.

We offer several different types of treatment, such as hands-on manual therapy, pain management modalities, and dry needling treatments to make sure you meet your physical therapy goals.

virginia licensed physical therapists

All of our physical therapists are licensed by the Virginia Board of Physical Therapy and are Direct Access Certified, so you do not need a referral or prescription to be evaluated or treated through our clinic.
